Service Download 

This button is used to begin the map service download process. Prior to pressing this button users should specify the ArcGIS connection, services, and layers they want to download using the MapServices Setup button. After making those selections the user needs to zoom into an area of interest and then press this button. All layers selected within the setup services form will be downloaded for the extent of the area of interest. While downloading, a progress form will appear indicating what has been downloaded and how much remains to be downloaded.
